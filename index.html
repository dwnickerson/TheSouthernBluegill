Our Association

Built by Anglers,
Grounded in Science

The Southern Bluegill Association was founded in November 2025 on a simple conviction: Southern waters deserve better tools than national averages and generic forecasts can provide.

Our lakes, ponds, rivers, and impoundments behave in ways unique to this region shaped by climate, geography, and the rhythms of Southern seasons. We model them individually, predict them precisely, and translate that data into guidance anglers can actually use.

We are early in our work. But the tools are live, the science is sound, and the mission is clear.

What We've Built

Two Tools, One Purpose

Tool 01    Water Science

Water Temperature
Estimation Model

Model a specific body of water not a regional estimate and receive localized water temperature predictions along with environmental advisories. The model accounts for air temperature, wind, recent trends, and water dynamics to estimate future conditions with genuine accuracy.

  • Site-specific water temperature forecasting
  • Fish kill risk advisories & early warnings
  • Thermal trend analysis for Southern impoundments
  • Customizable to individual lakes & ponds
Open the Model  
Tool 02    Fishing Forecast

FishCast
Fishing Intelligence

Location- and species-specific fishing forecasts that go far beyond generic ratings. Specify your water and target species and FishCast returns detailed bite predictions, optimal timing windows, and conditions analysis drawn from real environmental data.

  • Species-specific bite forecasting
  • Best fishing times by hour of day
  • Water temperature & weather integration
  • Multiple favorites for quick daily checks
