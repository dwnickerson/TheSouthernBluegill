THE SOUTHERN BLUEGILL ASSOCIATION – OFFICIALLY LAUNCHED!

D.W. Published on November 26, 2025

 I’m jumping in head-first and have no idea what I’m doing — but that’s exactly why I decided to form an LLC just to figure out how hard (or easy) it actually is, and what real benefits come with it.

I wanted this to be about something I genuinely love, so I picked one of my favorite hobbies: chasing bluegill, redbreast, pumpkinseeds, bream, brim, sunnies, chinquapin, redear, shellcrackers, perch… whatever you call those scrappy little fighters where you’re from. There’s nothing more satisfying than a cork going under, a bent ultralight rod or a battle on a fly rod.

This space is going to be pretty simple: I’ll share my own fishing stories, photos, favorite tactics, and links to the best resources I find along the way. We’ll see where it takes us.
